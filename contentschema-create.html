Content Schema - Create

Download .pdf


This assignment was a content schema designed for adding characters to a tactical role-playing video game, roughly modeled after a video game development project. Developing a schema in which one of the anticipated users is a computer turned out to pose interesting challenges.

Pushing this schema further during revisions, I incorporated faceted classification, controlled vocabularies, and layers of interdependency between attributes. To try and make the schema more intuitive to humans, I also experimented with color coding and notation.
